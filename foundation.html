Our Vision

To honor the life and legacy of Alan Zheng by empowering young people to harness data science, artificial intelligence, and technology for the good of humanity and the planet.

Our Mission

The Alan Zheng Foundation is dedicated to continuing Alan's dream of using innovation and compassion to address global challenges. Through scholarships, research support, and educational outreach, we seek to inspire and equip the next generation of students to combine technical excellence with a deep sense of responsibility toward the environment and society.
